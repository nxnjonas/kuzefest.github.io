Lights Festival of Santiago 2017

KÜZEFEST

October 2017
18 - 21

STGO Chile

"Küzefest, the first light festival in Santiago de Chile, is an event in which artists, lighting designers, audio-visual specialists and citizens unite to rediscover and enchant the city through light. In this version, video projections of cartography on facades of buildings and light interventions take center stage. The theme of this version is the power of nature." Küzefest is free and open. Developed between October 18 and 21, 2017
